# Aire It Out — Lifecycle Email Sequence

Tone: warm, personal, human. Like a message from the founder, not a marketing department.

---

## Email 1 — Day 0: Welcome

**Subject:** your first word

**Body:**

Hey,

Welcome to Aire It Out. I'm glad you're here.

The whole idea behind this app is simple: speaking is more natural than writing. You know things out loud that you don't know on paper. We wanted to make a space for that.

So here's how to start: just press record. Say whatever's on your mind — something from today, something you've been carrying, something you can't quite name yet. You don't have to be eloquent. You don't have to know what you feel before you start.

After you record, you'll get a gentle AI reflection — not advice, just a quiet mirror of what you said.

That's it. That's your first entry.

Try it tonight. Even two minutes.

— The Aire It Out team

P.S. If you want some structure, tap "Morning Intention" in the app tomorrow morning. One question, one sentence. It's the smallest ritual that somehow changes everything.

## Email 2 — Day 3: Check-In

**Subject:** how's it going?

**Body:**

Hey,

Three days in. How's it feeling?

We've heard from a lot of people that the first entry is the hardest — not because of anything in the app, but because saying something out loud to yourself is weirdly vulnerable. If that's where you are, that's normal.

A few things that help:
- **Start small.** You don't need to say something profound. "Today felt long and I don't totally know why" is a complete entry.
- **Morning Intention.** If evenings feel hard, try the morning instead. One question, right after you wake up, before you open anything else.
- **Let it be messy.** The AI reflection works best when you're not performing. Just talk.

You've got this. We're rooting for you.

— Aire It Out

**Subject:** when you need to go deeper

**Body:**

Hey,

By now you've had a week of entries. Your night sky is starting to fill in.

This week, we wanted to show you something that some of our favorite users love most: **Star Sessions**.

A Star Session is a guided reflection experience — you choose a theme (grief, gratitude, identity, change), answer 4 voice prompts, and receive a personal AI reflection when you're done.

It's not journaling. It's more like a conversation with yourself — structured just enough to help you go somewhere you wouldn't go alone.

Here's how to start: tap "Star Sessions" in the app → choose a session → answer the prompts in order → see what the AI reflects back.

Some people do one a week. Some do one a month. There's no right answer.

*(Star Sessions are a Pro feature. If you haven't tried Pro yet, you get a 7-day free trial — no charge until the trial ends.)*

Keep going,
