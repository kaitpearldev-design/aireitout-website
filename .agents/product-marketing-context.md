## Product Overview

**Name:** Aire It Out
**Tagline:** Say it. Feel it. Let go.
**Category:** Voice journaling / emotional wellness
**Platforms:** iOS, Android (free with Pro subscription)
**Price:** Free tier + Pro ($4.99/mo or $39.99/yr, 7-day trial)
**Website:** aireitout.app
**Support:** support@aireitout.app

**Core value proposition:**
Journaling is hard when you stare at a blank page. Speaking is natural — you've been doing it your whole life. Aire It Out lets you record your voice, get gentle AI reflections, and watch your memories become stars in a personal night sky.

## What It Does (Feature Summary)

**Free:**
- Unlimited voice recordings
- Morning Intention & Evening Reflection (daily guided check-ins)
- AI Reflection (3/day — AI reflects back what you said)
- 10 mood colors + tracking
- Daily streaks & achievements
- Time Capsules (7, 30, 90-day locks)
- Entry sharing with expiring links
- Night Sky (limited)
- App Lock (PIN, Face ID)
- CSV export + single-entry PDF

**Pro:**
- Unlimited AI Reflections
- Voice transcription (text from audio)
- Star Sessions (guided deep-reflection with 4 voice prompts + AI summary)
- Weekly Mood Insights (AI narrative of emotional week)
- Monthly Photo Album (vintage Polaroid-style)
- Custom Time Capsules (any future date)
- Wave Breathing (3 guided patterns)
- Full Night Sky
- Memoir-style PDF + monthly PDF export

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Adults 25–45 who want to journal but struggle to start or maintain the habit.

**Secondary:** People in therapy or wellness journeys who want a daily practice between sessions.

**Who they are:**
- Busy — journaling always feels like "one more thing"
- Reflective — they *want* to understand themselves better
- Already talk to themselves (in the shower, car, walks)
- Have tried journaling apps, found them intimidating or boring
- More comfortable speaking than writing

---

## Personas

**The Anxious Overthinker**
Lives in their head. Has a lot to process. Writing feels like it requires too much organization. Speaking lets them ramble and figure it out along the way. Loves the AI reflection because it makes sense of what they said.

**The Creative Soul**
Journals in bursts but can't maintain daily habits. Loves the Night Sky metaphor. Attracted to the poetic aesthetic. Does Star Sessions for creative inspiration.

**The Habit Builder**
Goal-oriented, uses streaks. Wants a structured morning ritual. Morning Intention + Evening Reflection is their anchor. Loves the achievements and streak tracking.

---

## Problems We Solve

1. **Blank page anxiety** — no text field to stare at, just a record button
2. **Journaling feels like homework** — speaking is faster, easier, more natural
3. **Not knowing how you feel** — speaking it out loud helps you figure it out in real time
4. **Forgetting important moments** — entries become stars in a permanent night sky
5. **Shallow journaling** — Star Sessions and AI reflections push you deeper
6. **Inconsistency** — streaks, Morning Intention, Evening Reflection build the habit

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Warm. Poetic. Human. A wise friend who believes in the power of speaking your truth.

**Not:** Corporate, clinical, self-help-speak, productivity-bro, overly optimistic.

**Writing principles:**
- Short sentences. Active voice.
- Benefit-first, not feature-first.
- Emotion before logic.
- Second person ("you") always.
- Avoid: "leverage", "optimize", "supercharge", "unlock your potential"
- Use: "speak", "breathe", "remember", "let go", "find", "carry", "hold"

**Examples of on-brand copy:**
✓ "Say it. Feel it. Let go."
✓ "Your voice knows things your typing doesn't."
✓ "The blank page never asked you how you're doing. We do."
✓ "Some things need to be said out loud before they make sense."
